# Pair WhatsApp

Connecting WednesdayAI to WhatsApp takes about a minute. You scan a QR code on your phone - no phone number entry, no verification code.

## What you need

- A smartphone with WhatsApp installed
- Your administrator's confirmation that the WhatsApp channel is set up

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Ask your administrator for the QR code">
    Your administrator can show the pairing QR code by running:

    ```bash
    openclaw channels whatsapp qr
    ```

    This displays a QR code in the terminal, or they can share it with you as an image.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Scan the QR code on your phone">
    On your phone:

    1. Open WhatsApp
    2. Tap the **three-dot menu** (Android) or **Settings** (iPhone)
    3. Tap **Linked devices**
    4. Tap **Link a device**
    5. Scan the QR code your administrator showed you

    You will see "Device successfully linked" in WhatsApp.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Say hello">
    WednesdayAI will send you a welcome message in WhatsApp within a few seconds of linking.

    If nothing arrives after 30 seconds, send a message yourself:

    > Hi

    This confirms the connection is working.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## In a group? Mention the bot

If the bot is in a WhatsApp group, it does **not** answer every message - it stays quiet until you **ping it**. To get its attention:

- Tap to @-mention it, or type its name, or include its number in your message
- Or reply to one of its previous messages

Each group has its own private session, so what you say in a group does not mix with your one-on-one chat.

## Tips

- **WhatsApp must stay connected** - your phone needs an internet connection for messages to reach WednesdayAI. The gateway buffers messages while your phone is briefly offline, but long offline periods may cause delays.
- **You do not need to keep WhatsApp open** - background delivery works as long as your phone has a data connection.
- **Re-linking** - if your phone is factory reset or WhatsApp is reinstalled, you will need to re-scan the QR code. Ask your administrator.

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="QR code expired before I could scan it">
    QR codes expire quickly. Ask your administrator to run the QR command again to get a fresh code.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Welcome message never arrived">
    Check that your phone has a data connection. If it does, ask your administrator to run `openclaw doctor` to check the WhatsApp channel status.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="WhatsApp says the session expired">
    You have been away for a long time and the session timed out. Ask your administrator to generate a new QR code and re-scan it.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="The bot ignores me in a group">
    In groups the bot only replies when mentioned, pinged, or replied to. Make sure you are pinging it. If it still does not respond, the group may not be allowed yet - ask your administrator.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="My messages are going to the wrong AI or are not being answered">
    Your administrator may need to add your WhatsApp number to the allowed contacts list. Ask them to check the channel access policy.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
