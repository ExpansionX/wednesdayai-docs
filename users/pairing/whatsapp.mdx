# Pair WhatsApp

Connecting WednesdayAI to WhatsApp takes about a minute. You scan a QR code on your phone — no phone number entry, no verification code.

## What you need

- A smartphone with WhatsApp installed
- Your administrator's confirmation that the WhatsApp channel is set up

## Steps

### 1. Ask your administrator for the QR code

Your administrator can show the pairing QR code by running:

```bash
openclaw channels whatsapp qr
```

This displays a QR code in the terminal, or they can share it with you as an image.

### 2. Scan the QR code on your phone

On your phone:

1. Open WhatsApp
2. Tap the **three-dot menu** (Android) or **Settings** (iPhone)
3. Tap **Linked devices**
4. Tap **Link a device**
5. Scan the QR code your administrator showed you

You'll see "Device successfully linked" in WhatsApp.

### 3. Say hello

WednesdayAI will send you a welcome message in WhatsApp within a few seconds of linking.

If you don't receive a welcome message after 30 seconds, send a message yourself:

> Hi

This confirms the connection is working.

## Tips

- **WhatsApp must stay connected** — your phone needs an internet connection for messages to reach WednesdayAI. The gateway buffers messages while your phone is briefly offline, but extended offline periods may cause delays.
- **You don't need to keep WhatsApp open** — background delivery works as long as your phone has a data connection.
- **Re-linking** — if your phone is factory reset or WhatsApp is reinstalled, you'll need to re-scan the QR code. Ask your administrator.

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="QR code expired before I could scan it">
    QR codes expire after 20 seconds. Ask your administrator to run the QR command again to get a fresh code.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Welcome message never arrived">
    Check that your phone has a data connection. If it does, ask your administrator to run `openclaw doctor` to check the WhatsApp channel status.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="WhatsApp says the session expired">
    You have been away for a long time and the session timed out. Ask your administrator to generate a new QR code and re-scan it.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="My messages are going to the wrong AI or aren't being answered">
    Your administrator may need to add your WhatsApp number to the allowed contacts list. Ask them to check the channel access policy.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
