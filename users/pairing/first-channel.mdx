WednesdayAI works inside the apps you already use. This guide walks you through connecting your first messaging app.

## What "pairing" means

When you pair a channel, WednesdayAI learns to recognise messages from you on that channel and route your conversations to your AI. It is a one-time setup step.

After pairing:
- Your messages in the channel reach your AI instantly
- Your conversation history is private to you
- You can use all [slash commands](/users/commands) and [built-in tools](/users/tools/index)

## Before you start

Make sure your administrator has already set up the WednesdayAI gateway and the channel you want to use. If you are not sure, send a message to your administrator or check the [admin docs](/admin/channels/index).

## After pairing

Once connected, send a message to your AI to say hello. Try:

> "Hi! What can you do?"

Your AI will introduce itself and describe what it can help with.

To pair a second channel later, follow the same steps for that channel. Your conversation history and settings carry over.
