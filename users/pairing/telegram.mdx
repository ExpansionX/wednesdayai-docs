# Pair Telegram

Connecting WednesdayAI on Telegram is the simplest pairing of any channel — there is no QR code to scan. You just find the bot your administrator set up and send it a message.

## What you need

- Telegram installed on your phone or computer
- The bot username from your administrator (e.g. `@MyAIBot`)

## Steps

### 1. Get the bot username from your administrator

Your administrator set up a Telegram bot when configuring WednesdayAI. Ask them for the bot's username — it starts with `@` and ends in `Bot`.

### 2. Open a chat with the bot

In Telegram, tap the search icon and type the bot username. Select it and tap **Start**.

Or open this link (replace `YourBotName` with the actual username):

```
https://t.me/YourBotName
```

### 3. Send your first message

Tap **Start** in the chat, or just type a message. Your AI will respond within a few seconds.

That's it — you're connected.

## Group chats

If your administrator has added the bot to a Telegram group, you can chat with your AI in that group too. To get the AI's attention in a group, either:

- Mention the bot by username: `@YourBotName what is the weather today?`
- Reply to one of the bot's previous messages

## Tips

- **Notifications** — make sure Telegram notifications are enabled so you see replies on your phone.
- **Multiple devices** — Telegram works across all your devices simultaneously. You can start a conversation on your phone and continue on desktop.

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="The bot doesn't respond">
    Make sure you tapped **Start** before sending a message — Telegram requires this for bots. If you already tapped Start and there's still no reply, ask your administrator to run `openclaw doctor` to check the Telegram channel status.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="I can't find the bot">
    Double-check the username with your administrator. Bot usernames are case-insensitive but must match exactly. Try searching without the `@` symbol.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="The bot says I'm not allowed">
    Your Telegram account ID may not be in the allowed users list. Ask your administrator to add you.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Messages in a group are ignored">
    In group chats, you need to mention the bot by username or reply to its messages. The bot won't respond to every message in a busy group by default.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
